Our Story

1. Starting Point

Since 2010 in Akihabara, Japan — known as the capital of anime culture — we founded EKOH to satisfy our love for anime songs. What started as a small project among a few passionate fans has now grown into a community where people from all over the world can experience the joy, nostalgia, and energy that anime music brings.

1. Starting Point

Since 2010 in Akihabara, Japan — known as the capital of anime culture — we founded EKOH to satisfy our love for anime songs. What started as a small project among a few passionate fans has now grown into a community where people from all over the world can experience the joy, nostalgia, and energy that anime music brings.

2. Expanding Beyond Borders

From our roots in Akihabara, EKOH has grown into a bridge connecting anime music fans around the world. What began as a small circle of enthusiasts has evolved into a global community, united by the rhythm, emotion, and storytelling found within every anime song.

EKOH History

2010

EKOH was founded by Itsuki Minami, whose vision was to transform a lifelong love for anime songs into a platform that connects fans and keeps the spirit of anime culture alive through music.

2011

EKOH opened its first official store in Akihabara, Japan — the heart of anime culture — creating a space where fans can experience, collect, and celebrate the music that defines their favorite worlds.

2013

It was during the golden age of anime culture that EKOH’s sales truly exploded, driven by a growing community of fans who found their identity and nostalgia in every song we released to customer.

2014

In the following year, we brought EKOH beyond Japan’s borders, officially turning it into an international brand that connects anime music fans worldwide.

2015 - NOW

EKOH is still growing, bringing high-quality anime soundtracks to fans around the world while staying true to the passion and creativity that started it all.
